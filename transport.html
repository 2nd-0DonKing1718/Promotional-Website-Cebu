Getting Around the Queen City

Jeepneys

The cultural icon of PH transport. Best for short distances and experiencing local life.

Taxi & Ride-Hailing

Use Grab or local white taxis for air-conditioned comfort and direct point-to-point travel.

MyBus & Ceres

Modern buses connecting the airport to major malls and northern/southern provinces.

FastCraft & Ferries

Convenient sea travel from Pier 1 to nearby islands like Bohol or Camotes.

Habal-Habal

Motorcycle taxis perfect for reaching mountain attractions like Sirao or Tops.
