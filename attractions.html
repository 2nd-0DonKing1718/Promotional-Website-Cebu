Explore Cebu's Wonders

Magellan's Cross

A historical landmark marking the arrival of Christianity in the Philippines.

Simala Church

A castle-like monastery known for its miraculous reputation and grand architecture.

Oslob

Famous worldwide for whale shark watching and the beautiful Tumalog Falls.

Kawasan Falls

Stunning multi-layered waterfalls famous for turquoise water and canyoneering.

Osmeña Peak

The highest point in Cebu, offering a panoramic view of jagged green hills.

Malapascua Island

A world-class diving destination known for thresher shark sightings.

Fort San Pedro

The oldest military defense structure in the country, built by the Spanish.

Bantayan Island

Known for its crystal clear waters, white sand beaches, and peaceful atmosphere.

Moalboal

A paradise for snorkelers featuring the famous "Sardine Run" and sea turtles.

Mactan Island

Home to luxury resorts, the Mactan Shrine, and vibrant marine sanctuaries.
