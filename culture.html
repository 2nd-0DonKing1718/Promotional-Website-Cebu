Festivals & Heritage

Sinulog Festival

The grandest festival in the Philippines honoring the Señor Santo Niño with vibrant street dancing.

Kadaugan sa Mactan

A historical re-enactment of the Battle of Mactan, celebrating Datu Lapu-Lapu's victory.

Cebu City Charter Day

Celebrating the city's foundation and administrative independence every February 24th.

Pasko sa Sugbo

A month-long celebration of Christmas with grand light displays and cultural evening shows.

Kabanhawan Festival

The "Resurrection Festival" in Minglanilla featuring the joyful "Sugat" dance ritual.

Tuba Festival

Honoring Borbon's coconut wine industry and the traditional craft of tuba making.

Handicrafts & Design

Cebu is the design capital, famous for world-class furniture and handmade guitars.
