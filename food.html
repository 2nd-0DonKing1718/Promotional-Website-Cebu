Local Delicacies & Specialties

Lechon Cebu

The gold standard of roasted pig. Crunchy skin and juicy, flavored meat.

Sutukil

Sugba (Grilled), Tuwa (Stewed), and Kilaw (Raw in vinegar).

Ngohiong

Deep-fried vegetable rolls with five-spice powder.

Larang Soup

Spicy and sour seafood stew, a local favorite.

Pochero

Rich beef marrow soup with corn and vegetables.

Humba

Sweet and savory braised pork belly with black beans.

Bakasi Kinilaw

Fresh eel marinated in vinegar and local spices.

Bibingka Mandaue

Sticky rice cakes made with coconut sap (tuba).

Torta Cebuana

Rich, lard-based cupcakes flavored with anise.

Otap

Flaky, brittle puff pastry sprinkled with sugar.

Hopia

Traditional bean-filled mooncakes.

Puso

Hanging rice wrapped in coconut leaves.
